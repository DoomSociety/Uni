IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
       PROGRAM-ID. LZW-TREE.

       ENVIRONMENT DIVISION.
       INPUT-OUTPUT SECTION.
       FILE-CONTROL.
           SELECT IN-FILE ASSIGN TO EXTERNAL IN-FILENAME
           ORGANIZATION IS BINARY SEQUENTIAL.
           SELECT OUT-FILE ASSIGN TO EXTERNAL OUT-FILENAME
           ORGANIZATION IS LINE SEQUENTIAL.

       DATA DIVISION.
       FILE SECTION.
       FD IN-FILE.
       01 IN-BYTE PIC X.
       FD OUT-FILE.
       01 OUT-LINE PIC X(100).

       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
       01 IN-FILENAME PIC X(50).
       01 OUT-FILENAME PIC X(50).
       

       01 TREE-STRUCTURE.
          05 NODE-COUNT PIC 9(5) VALUE 1.
          05 NODES OCCURS 10000 TIMES INDEXED BY N-IDX.
             10 NODE-CHAR PIC X.
             10 LEFT-CHILD PIC 9(5) VALUE 0.
             10 RIGHT-CHILD PIC 9(5) VALUE 0.

       01 CURRENT-NODE PIC 9(5) VALUE 1.
       01 TEMP-BYTE PIC 9(3).
       01 BIT-IDX PIC 9.
       01 IN-EOF PIC X VALUE 'N'.
       01 COMMENT-FLAG PIC X VALUE 'N'.

       PROCEDURE DIVISION.
           ACCEPT IN-FILENAME FROM ARGUMENT-VALUE.
           MOVE "output.txt" TO OUT-FILENAME.

           OPEN INPUT IN-FILE.
           INITIALIZE TREE-STRUCTURE.
           MOVE '/' TO NODE-CHAR(1).

           PERFORM UNTIL IN-EOF = 'Y'
               READ IN-FILE INTO IN-BYTE
                   AT END MOVE 'Y' TO IN-EOF
                   NOT AT END
                       MOVE FUNCTION ORD(IN-BYTE) TO TEMP-BYTE
                       SUBTRACT 1 FROM TEMP-BYTE
                       PERFORM PROCESS-BITS
               END-READ
           END-PERFORM.

           CLOSE IN-FILE.
           STOP RUN.

       PROCESS-BITS.
           PERFORM VARYING BIT-IDX FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL BIT-IDX > 8
               IF TEMP-BYTE >= 128
                   PERFORM ADD-TO-TREE-1
                   COMPUTE TEMP-BYTE = (TEMP-BYTE - 128) * 2
               ELSE
                   PERFORM ADD-TO-TREE-0
                   COMPUTE TEMP-BYTE = TEMP-BYTE * 2
               END-IF
           END-PERFORM.

       ADD-TO-TREE-0.
           IF LEFT-CHILD(CURRENT-NODE) = 0
               ADD 1 TO NODE-COUNT
               MOVE NODE-COUNT TO LEFT-CHILD(CURRENT-NODE)
               MOVE '0' TO NODE-CHAR(NODE-COUNT)
               MOVE 1 TO CURRENT-NODE
           ELSE
               MOVE LEFT-CHILD(CURRENT-NODE) TO CURRENT-NODE
           END-IF.

       ADD-TO-TREE-1.
           IF RIGHT-CHILD(CURRENT-NODE) = 0
               ADD 1 TO NODE-COUNT
               MOVE NODE-COUNT TO RIGHT-CHILD(CURRENT-NODE)
               MOVE '1' TO NODE-CHAR(NODE-COUNT)
               MOVE 1 TO CURRENT-NODE
           ELSE
               MOVE RIGHT-CHILD(CURRENT-NODE) TO CURRENT-NODE
           END-IF.
